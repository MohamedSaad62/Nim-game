Rules of the Game of Nim

  • The game starts with several piles of objects (e.g., stones or sticks).
  • Two players take turns.
  • On each turn, a player must:
    • Choose one pile, and
    • Remove one or more objects from it.
  • Players must remove at least one object on their turn.
  • The player who removes the last object wins the game.
